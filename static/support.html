Installation

Once you've installed the Sanasolmu bot into your Slack workspace, invite it to a channel. It's best to dedicate a channel for this, to limit the bot's access to any possibly confidential messages on other channels.

Once Sanasolmu has joined a channel, the following command becomes available:

  • !knot — Starts a new round. Language and/or word length can be given as parameters, for example !knot fi 4 would start the game with a finnish four letter word.

The bot will then reply with a jumbled word, for example: T L O A.
